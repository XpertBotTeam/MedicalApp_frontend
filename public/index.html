About Us

MedHelp is a comprehensive mobile application and website designed to empower individuals in tracking and managing their medication schedules effectively. The platform offers a range of features to ensure medication adherence, safety, and personalized recommendations for each patient.

Our Services

Our Platform offers a range of features to ensure medication adherence, safety, and personalized recommendations for each patient.
Medication Reminders

The mobile app and website provide customizable medication reminders, ensuring that users never miss a dose. Users can set specific times for taking each medication, and the system sends notifications to their devices to prompt them accordingly.

Dosage Instructions

The system allows users to input their prescribed dosage instructions for each medication, including frequency and dosage amounts. This information is readily accessible for the user's reference.

Drug Interaction Checks

To ensure user safety, the system incorporates drug interaction checks. When multiple medications are entered into the system, it automatically cross-references them to identify potential drug interactions and alerts users and healthcare providers if any risks are detected.

OCR Prescription Scanning

Google Cloud Vision's Optical Character Recognition (OCR) capabilities are utilized to scan prescription labels. Users can simply capture an image of their prescription, and the system automatically extracts medication details, reducing manual data entry errors.

Personalized Medication Recommendations

The system employs machine learning algorithms that take into account the user's medical history, allergies, and past medication interactions to provide personalized medication recommendations. This enhances medication effectiveness and minimizes adverse effects.

Healthcare Provider Interface

The admin interface is designed for healthcare providers to access patient profiles securely. Providers can review medication regimens, monitor adherence levels, and intervene if necessary. This helps healthcare professionals offer personalized care and support to their patients remotely.
